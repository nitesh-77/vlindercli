# Why VlinderAI Exists

Big Tech's AI is presented as magic. emergent, inexplicable, unattainable. This mystification discourages understanding. If it's magic, you can revere it,be scared by it, and most importantly, only consume it.

Scratch the surface: it's engineering. Good engineering, but engineering. Understandable. Deconstructable. Buildable.

VlinderAI is my attempt to deconstruct the magic into clean domain models.

Well-engineered systems evoke mechanical sympathy. A beautifully made car makes you want to learn more about engines, buy a classic, spend weekends in the garage. The mental models reveal itself. You understand by using.
